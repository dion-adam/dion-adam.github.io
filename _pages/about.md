I am currently a first-year PhD student at the [SPIN Research Group](https://cgiannoula.github.io/pages/spin_home.html) of the [Max Planck Institute for Software Systems](https://www.mpi-sws.org/), where I work under the supervision of [Dr. Christina Giannoula](https://cgiannoula.github.io/pages/index.html). My research focuses on Systems for Machine Learning, with a particular interest in efficient serving and hardware/software co-design for emerging Machine Learning workloads. Previously, I obtained my Diploma (M.Eng. equivalent degree) in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the [National Technical University of Athens](https://www.ece.ntua.gr/en/).
